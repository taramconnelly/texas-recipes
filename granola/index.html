Homemade Granola

paleo and vegan, I think it tastes better than the oats.

Ingredients
  • 1 c coconut flakes
  • 1/4 c pumpkin seed (not from China)
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (grind fresh cinnamon stick)
  • 1/4 or more cup sunflower seed
  • 1/4 or a little more c quinoa (rinse with water)
  • 3 tsp coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup 100% real maple syrup
  • 4 big pinchs salt
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degree F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all dry ingredients (coconut flakes, pumpkin seed, cinnamon, sunflower seed, quinoa).
  3. In a small saucepan, heat coconut oil and maple syrup over medium-low heat just until coconut becomes liquid.
  4. Add liquid mix to dry mix, til it's all evenly covered.
  5. Line two baking sheets. Spread granola mix on thin thin layer. Make sure it's all very evenly flat.
  6. Place in oven for 20 minutes, gently stir and check ends half way through.
  7. As you bake at your granola, make sure it gets to a toasted, golden brown (and will smell amazingly aromatic!). Check your granola often so it does not burn.
  8. Take out, stir a little more, move the ends in the middle and the middle toward the end to cook evenly. Rotate sheets 180 so it's cooked as evenly as possible.
  9. Bake another 8 minutes.
  10. Take out, let sit to cool. Granola will get crispy when it cools down.
  11. Store in airtight container.
