Bobby's Chana Masala Recipe

Adapted in South Texas, 2022.

Serves 4. Medium Level Heat.

Difficulty level: Advanced. Vegetarian!

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp MDT Chan Masala powder (cumin, black salt, mint, fenugree leaf, mace nutmeg...)
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup chana (chickpea) (crossed out)
  • 3 bay leaf
  • 2 apple green (crossed out)
  • 1 tsp panch phoron (fenugreek, yellow mustard, fennel, nigella, cumin)--(crossed out)
  • to taste: honey
  • 2 tsp Jaggary (or brown sugar)
  • to taste: salt 3 tsp
  • ~ 3 onion
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Indian bay leaf
  • 6-8 (7) cardamon black
  • 10-12 (11) clove
  • 3/4 lb cherry tomato
  • 2 tsp dried pomengranite seeds (anardana)
  • 6-8 serrano chile
  • 10-12 1" pieces ginger
  • 8 key lime
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crossed out)
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 4 Tbsp tamarind (crossed out) (replace with apple stuff)
  • 1" cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Julliane ginger.
  2. In small stainless saucepan, heat butter. Once clarified, add bay, caradmon, clove, cinnamon. Cook on low-low (1).
  3. Slice onions (3) thin.
  4. Combine in dutch oven (onion & spice butter).
  5. Mix well, put top on, raise heat to low (3). Cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Slice serranos (7) into 1/4" or 1/2 cm slices.
  7. After onion is browned, add sliced serrano, on med (5) tomato, paprika (2 tsp), salt (3 tsp) cook with top on. At least 5 minutes.
  8. Add chana (chick pea) with liquid, sliced ginger, cook for 20 minutes. 11 1" pieces. Cook on med-low (4).
  9. Add chana masala (4 tsp) pomengranite seeds (2 tsp), sugar (2 tsp) apply chutney ( 1 apple) mango powder (1 tsp). Cook for 5 min on med (5).
  10. Add lime juice (6) and pinch of mace, pinch of nutmeg.
  11. Let cool.
  12. Honey to taste.
Robert Colson, famous chef.
