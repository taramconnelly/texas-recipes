Homemade (and mostly Organic) Hairspray Recipe

Copied from John Master's Organics hairspray (cost $25 for a tiny bottle!)

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon glycerin
  • 1 teaspoon gum arabic
  • 1/16 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 12 teaspoons distilled water
  • 3 teaspoons everclear
  • 6 drops bergamot essential oil

Steps

  1. Add glycerin to the gum arabic.
  2. Mix all with water.
